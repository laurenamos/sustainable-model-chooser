Sustainable Model Chooser v0
A prototype comparison site for everyday chat users. It scores models using verifiable inputs (pricing + policy transparency), and labels sustainability as proxy/unknown unless a vendor discloses it.

Does OpenRouter list sustainability / privacy metrics?
OpenRouter’s public model list primarily includes: pricing, context_length, input/output modalities, supported parameters, and a few provider flags (e.g. is_moderated). It does not provide reliable per-model CO2e, kWh, water usage, or retention/training policy fields. This site therefore links to vendor policy pages as citations.

Scoring (v0)
  • Affordability (40): proxy from published $/MTok. Assumes a typical chat turn of 1K input + 500 output tokens.
  • Privacy & data controls (40): scored on clarity + citations + stated controls. Not legal advice.
  • Sustainability transparency (20): focuses on disclosure + verifiability. We do not claim a precise CO2e per message.
